Columnar Cactus

The colors are beautiful, but the barrel cactus has an iner secret: if you are in need of hydration in the desert, the cactus has a lotof fluid inside and has saved the lives of many. Barrel cactus buds typically start to bloom in April with a bright yello of orange flower. Pink and red varieties also exist but occur less frequently. The flower only appear on the very top of the plant.
