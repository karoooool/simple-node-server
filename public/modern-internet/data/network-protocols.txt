<p>Network Protocol include guidelines that regulate the following characteristics of a network: access method, allowed physical topologies, types of cabling, and speed of data transfer.
</p>
<p>
The most common network protocols are:<br>
<blockquote>Ethernet</blockquote>
<blockquote>Local Talk</blockquote>
<blockquote>Token Ring</blockquote>
<blockquote>FDDI</blockquote>
<blockquote>ATM</blockquote>
