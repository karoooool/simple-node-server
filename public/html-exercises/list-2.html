Ingredients

For crust

  • 2 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar

For filling

  • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 16-ounce container sour cream
  • 1/4 milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

For topping

  • 2 6-ounce baskets fresh bluebrries
  • 1/3 cup all-fruit blueberry spread

Preparation

Make crust

  1. Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375 F.
  2. Wrap outside of 10-inch-diameter springform pan with heavy-duty foil.
  3. Press crumb mixture firmly onto bottom and 2 inches up sides of prepared pan.
  4. Bake until crust begins to brown, about 8 minutes, and transfer crust to rack and cool.

Make filling

  1. Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar in large bowl until well blended
  2. Beat in flour and eggs 1 at a time, beating just until combined.
  3. Beat in sour cream, milk and vanilla.
  4. Pour filling into crust.
  5. Place springform pan in large roasting pan.
  6. Pour enough hot water into roasting pan to come 1 inch side of pan.
  7. Bake cheesecake until just set in center and top is slightly puffed and golden brown.
  8. Turn off oven; keep door closed; let stand in oven for 1 hour.
  9. Remove cheesecake from roasting pan and refrigerate it until cold.
  10. Cover;refrigerate overnight.

Make topping

  1. Stir blueberry spread in large skillet over low heat until melted.
  2. Remove from heat and add blueberries.
  3. Spoon blueberry over top of cake, spreading evenly.
  4. Chill until cold annd transfer cke to platter.
